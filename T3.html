Grilled Sandwich

List of ingredients

  • bacon
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Bread

Instructions

  • Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy.
  • Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  • Place two slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  • Add a slice of cheese and some tomato slices to each sandwich.
  • Add a slice of cooked bacon on top of each sandwich.
  • Top each sandwich with another slice of bread, butter side up.
  • Cook the sandwiches until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
